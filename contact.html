Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

You can book a tour through our website by selecting your desired tour and filling out the booking form. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team for assistance.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation policies. You can cancel your booking up to 30 days before the tour date for a full refund. For cancellations within 30 days, please contact our customer service team.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special discounts for groups of 10 or more people. Please contact our sales team for more information about group bookings and discounts.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For group bookings, we also offer flexible payment plans.
